Aluminum or wood finish

We offer four distinctive exterior finishes for your luggage. For a minimalist and modern look, you can choose between a light grey or black aluminum finish. Or for a more exceptional travel piece, select your noble wood of your choice (maple, cherrywood), that will be crafted by our luthier.

Leather color

All our luggage exterior linings and atraps are made with young bull leather, sourced from a French family tannery founded in 1927. This leather is strong yet soft and elegant, guaranteeing exeptional wear for everyday use. With a selection of 50 shades, the leather color is the perfect opportunity to express your style.

Alcantara® color

We use a luxurious avant-garde fabric to cover the luggage interior. Alcantara®, made in ltaly, is a sophisticated material, like a soft suede. In addition to being water repellent, Alcantara® is eco-friendly. You cna select any color from a nearly limitless palette to create your own interior look.

Monogram engraving

All our luggage is engraved in the interior with a unique serial number and the owner's name. Our bespoke service offers an additional monogram engraving on the handle, a final personal touch!
